**Interval**

 Interval in seconds for updating placeholders.

*Default value:*

 

    interval: 0.5
   
   ---
   **View Distance**
   
   The maximum distance at which the nametag will be visible, in blocks.

*Default Value:*

    view_distance: 64

---
**Config Version**

Do not change this value!
